Ministerial Departments

Cabinet office

Led by Tombo himself, the Cabinet Office is a key department in the government of the Republic of Goddard, responsible for supporting the work of the Prime Minister and Cabinet, and coordinating the work of other government departments.

Department for Business and Economics

The Department for Business and Economics is a vital government department in the Republic of Goddard, tasked with shaping the country's economic and business policy.

Department for Education

The Department for Education oversees the education system in the Republic of Goddard. The department is led by the Minister for Education, who is responsible for setting the direction and priorities for education policy in the country.

Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs

The Department for Environment, Food, and Rural Affairs is responsible for protecting and enhancing the natural environment, promoting sustainable food production and consumption, and supporting rural communities in the Republic of Goddard.

Department for International Trade

The Department for International Trade is responsible for promoting the Republic of Goddard's trade and investment interests around the world. The department is led by the Minister for International Trade, who is responsible for setting the direction and priorities for trade policy in the country.

Department for Technological Advancement, Digital Culture and Media

The Department for Technological Advancement, Digital Culture, and Media is responsible for promoting the Republic of Goddard's technological innovation, digital literacy, and cultural vibrancy.

Department for Transport

The Department for Transport is responsible for overseeing the transportation system in the Republic of Goddard. The department is led by the Minister for Transport, who is responsible for setting the direction and priorities for transport policy in the country.

Department for Health and Social Care

The Department for Health and Social Care is responsible for overseeing the health and social care system in the Republic of Goddard. The department is led by the Minister for Health and Social Care, who is responsible for setting the direction and priorities for health and social care policy in the country.

Ministry for National Defence and Military

The Ministry for National Defence and Military is responsible for overseeing the national defense and security of the Republic of Goddard. The ministry is led by the Minister for National Defence and Military, who is responsible for setting the direction and priorities for defense and security policy in the country.

Office of the Leader of the House of Commons

The Office of the Leader of the House of Commons is responsible for supporting the Leader of the House of Commons in their duties and responsibilities. The Leader of the House of Commons is a senior member of the government, responsible for coordinating the business of the House of Commons and representing the government in the House.

Office of the Leader of the House of Lords

The Office of the Leader of the House of Lords is responsible for supporting the Leader of the House of Lords in their duties and responsibilities. The Leader of the House of Lords is a senior member of the government, responsible for coordinating the business of the House of Lords and representing the government in the House.
